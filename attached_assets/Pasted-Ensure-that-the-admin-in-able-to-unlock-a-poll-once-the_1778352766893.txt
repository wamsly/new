These records should include
 1. Election Results Report
Shows official election outcomes and winners. Includes election details, positions contested, candidate results (votes, percentages, rankings), winner declaration, voting statistics, and audit information such as generation date and election ID.
2. Voter Turnout Report
Measures voter participation and engagement. Includes total registered voters, total voters who participated, turnout percentages, participation breakdown by faculty/department/campus, voting trends over time, and election metadata.
3. Candidate Report
Provides records of approved candidates. Includes candidate personal details, position contested, campaign information (manifesto, slogan, bio), uploaded verification documents, approval details, and candidate status such as approved, pending, rejected, or disqualified.
4. Voter Participation Report
Tracks voter activity for auditing purposes without revealing vote choices. Includes voter details, voting status, voting timestamps, authentication information, participation statistics, and suspicious activity indicators such as failed OTP attempts or duplicate login attempts.
5. Rejected Candidates Report
Provides accountability for rejected or disqualified applicants. Includes applicant details, position applied for, rejection reasons, review information, supporting evidence, and application timeline from submission to rejection.
